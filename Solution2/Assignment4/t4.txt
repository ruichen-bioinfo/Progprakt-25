CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the restart of stalled replication forks, which
CC       reloads the replicative helicase on sites other than the origin of
CC       replication; the PriA-PriB pathway is the major replication restart
CC       pathway. During primosome assembly it facilitates complex formation
CC       between PriA and DnaT on DNA; stabilizes PriA on DNA. Stimulates the
CC       DNA unwinding activity of PriA helicase. {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-
CC       Rule:MF_00720}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. Interacts with PriA and DnaT. Component of the
CC       replication restart primosome. Primosome assembly occurs via a 'hand-
CC       off' mechanism. PriA binds to replication forks, subsequently PriB then
CC       DnaT bind; DnaT then displaces ssDNA to generate the helicase loading
CC       substrate. {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-Rule:MF_00720}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the PriB family. {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-
